    ` ``bash

    4. Math EquationsÂ¶

    The alignment score is calculated as:

$$
S = \sum_{i=1}^{n} s(a_i, b_i) - \sum_{j=1}^{m} g_j
$$

where $s$ is the substitution score and $g$ is the gap penalty.
